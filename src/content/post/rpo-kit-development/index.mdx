Leading engineer on the RPO Kit 🚀. As part of this project, I've worked on:

- High Precision Orbit Propagator (see [Simulator](/work/simulator))
- Filter development: 
  - EKF and UKF for both absolute orbit determination, and relative orbit determination. Dual-inertial/inertial-relative architectures, translational/rotational architectures combined. 
  - Development of filters for quaternions in Rodrigues parameters space! 
- Waypoint transfer algorithms.
- Convex-MPC in Relative Orbital Element formulation. 
- Collision Risk Probability algorithms.

Feel free to reach out to discuss! We'll be publishing on some of the novel technology soon — look out for that 👀! Until then, it's all confidential, so I must keep it vague.
