Lead engineer on the dynamics engine powering our company's simulator. Fully proprietary, with state-of-the-art accuracy validated against historical mission data.

#### Specs 

Integrator: range of choice between Runge-Kutta-Fehlberg 7(8) (recommended), lower order Runge-Kutta options, and others.

Force Model:
- gravity field of the Earth (GGM03C model) - choose number of harmonics.
- gravity of the solar system planets (JPLDE440)
- drag effect using NRLMSISE-00
- solar radiation pressure using a conical, geometrical, or cylindrical shadow model
- solid Earth tides (IERS Conventions 2010)
- ocean tides
- general relativity
- ECEF2ECI and ECI2ECEF transformations using IAU 2006 Resolutions
- Runge-Kutta-Munthe-Kaas Quaternion Integrator (no normalisation error).
- Built-in attitude controller/torque input available. 
- Thruster inputs
- Custom perturbation inputs
