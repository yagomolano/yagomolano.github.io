This project was to look at the feasibility of a mission to fly in very low earth orbit (VLEO) using current existing technology. It was also for a synthetic aperture radar mission. Something that was discovered during this project was that there are many special requirements when combining SAR and VLEO in terms of optimum shape and size, that is not very easy to understand like it may be for more traditional designs. Because of this, a model-based optimisation approach was devised that looked to model all subcomponents, so that the "optimum" minimum mass design could be found.
