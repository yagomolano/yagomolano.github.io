This project explored the feasibility of a VLEO synthetic aperture radar (SAR) mission using only TRL-9 (flight-ready) technology.

Combining SAR with VLEO introduces tough trade-offs in shape and sizing — far harder to untangle than in traditional satellite design. To handle this, I built a model-based optimisation framework that parametrises all subsystems, including the overall shape based on drag, to find the minimum-mass design.

The results were compelling enough for [ST Engineering Satellite Systems](https://www.stengg.com/en/satellite-systems/) to move forward with the project. The novel method was presented at the 2024 IEEE Aerospace Conference in Yellowstone. The pre-print is below!
