## My Life Story
I grew up fascinated by the "big questions" — sci-fi books, stars, and what the future could look like. That curiosity led me to Aerospace Engineering at **Imperial College London**, where a love for space travel stories turned into a career building the mathematical foundations and robotic systems that make them possible.
