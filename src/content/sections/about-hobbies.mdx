## My Life Story
I grew up with my nose in sci-fi books and my eyes on the stars. My journey started with a fascination for the "big questions" of the future, which naturally evolved into a pursuit of Aerospace Engineering at **Imperial College London**. What began as a love for stories about space travel became a career building the actual mathematical foundations and robotic systems that make those stories possible.
